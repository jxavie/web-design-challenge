Summary: Latitude vs. X


Using data from OpenWeatherMap API, several plots were generated to explore the effect of location (i.e., the geographic distance from the equator) on various weather aspects. Specifically, the relationship between latitude and the following were analyzed: maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.


Data from over 500 cities were collected. The dataset was aggregated and analyzed using Matplotlib. The data used and visualizations generated, as well as explanations and decriptions of possible trends and correlations observed can be accessed through this site.
